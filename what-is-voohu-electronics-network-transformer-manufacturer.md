## What Is VOOHU Electronics?

**VOOHU Electronics Technology Co., Ltd.** is a China-based manufacturer of precision magnetic components, focusing on **network transformers** (also known as Ethernet transformers or LAN transformers), Power over Ethernet (PoE) magnetics, common-mode chokes, and custom inductors.

The company serves three primary markets:
- **Industrial** – Ethernet switches, PLCs, industrial gateways, and factory automation
- **Automotive** – 100BASE-T1 and 1000BASE-T1 in-vehicle networks, ADAS, infotainment, and zone controllers
- **Communication** – Base stations, access points, and telecom infrastructure

Unlike broad-line component distributors, VOOHU focuses on **application-specific engineering support** — helping customers select the right transformer for their PHY, PoE class, temperature range, and EMI requirements.

## Core Products: Network Transformers for Demanding Environments

VOOHU's product portfolio is built around three main categories of network transformers.

### 1. Gigabit Ethernet Transformers (1000BASE-T)

Designed for industrial switches, IP cameras, and network interfaces that require:
- 1 Gbps data rate with low insertion loss (<1dB at 100MHz)
- Return loss better than -16dB for signal integrity
- PoE/PoE+/PoE++ compatibility (up to 90W)
- Industrial temperature range (-40°C to +85°C)

These components are available in surface-mount (SMT) packages for automated assembly.

### 2. Automotive Ethernet Transformers (100BASE-T1 / 1000BASE-T1)

Specifically engineered for in-vehicle networks with requirements that differ from industrial Ethernet:
- Single twisted pair (saves weight and cost in vehicle harnesses)
- Lower PSD (Power Spectral Density) for reduced EMI
- -40°C to +125°C operation (engine bay, roof, door modules)
- AEC-Q200 readiness for qualified automotive projects

Common applications include ADAS cameras, domain controllers, and zone architectures.

### 3. PoE Magnetics (Power over Ethernet Transformers)

Integrated magnetic modules that combine isolation transformer, common-mode choke, and auto-transformer:
- Support for PoE (802.3af, 350mA), PoE+ (802.3at, 600mA), and PoE++ (802.3bt, up to 1.2A)
- Designed to handle DC bias without core saturation
- Available in single-port and multi-port configurations
